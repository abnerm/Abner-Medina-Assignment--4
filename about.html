J. M. W. Turner

1775-1851

Joseph Mallord William Turner

Born in London, England. Turner, even at his early age was said to be headed for great success as a painter. His skill is unique and he is on the favorites in British History. He is mostly known for his great talent with color and light. One of very few painters to really personalize his paintings by getting his hand dirty with paint, which was something that was frowned upon in his time. Turner was one to not care about what critics thought was correct

Turner is of great inspiration in many areas. Turner enjoyed what he did and was never afraid of painting with his own personal. He grew up in a rought atmosphere. That aided to his own uniqueness.
